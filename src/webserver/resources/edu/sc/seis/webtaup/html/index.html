TauP Online Calculator:

Calculate times, paths and more interactively.

Examples:

  • describe phase: phase?distdeg=35&evdepth=100&phases=P,S
  • time: time?distdeg=35&evdepth=100&phases=P,S
  • pierce: pierce?distdeg=35&evdepth=100&phases=P,S&format=text
  • path: path?distdeg=35&evdepth=100&phases=P,S&format=svg
  • wavefront: wavefront?distdeg=35&evdepth=100&phases=P&format=svg
  • curve: curve?evdepth=100&phases=P&format=svg
  • scatter: path?scatter=200,20&distdeg=35&evdepth=100&phases=P,S,PoP,SOp&format=svg
  • reflection and transmission: http://www.seis.sc.edu/webtaup/refltrans?&topvp=6.5&topvs=3.75&topden=2.9&botvp=8&botvs=4.5&botden=3.3&anglestep=1&indown=true&absolute=true&pwave=true&xslowness=true&format=svg
  • phase amplitude curves: http://www.seis.sc.edu/webtaup/xy?model=iasp91&evdepth=0&phases=S,SKS,SKIKS&stadepth=0&xaxis=rayparam&yaxis=amp&format=svg
