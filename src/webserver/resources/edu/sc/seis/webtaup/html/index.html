TauP Online Calculator:

Calculate times, paths and more interactively.

Examples:

  • describe phase: phase?evdepth=100&phase=P,S
  • time: time?degree=35&evdepth=100&phase=P,S
  • pierce: pierce?degree=35&evdepth=100&phase=P,S&format=text
  • path: path?degree=35&evdepth=100&phase=P,S&format=svg
  • wavefront: wavefront?degree=35&evdepth=100&phase=P&format=svg
  • curve: curve?evdepth=100&phase=P&format=svg
  • scatter: path?scatter=200,20°ree=35&evdepth=100&phase=P,S,PoP,SOp&format=svg
  • reflection and transmission: refltrans?&layer=6.5,3.75,2.9,8,4.5,3.3&anglestep=1&down=true&abs=true&pwave=true&x=rayparam&legend=true&format=svg
  • phase amplitude curves: curve?model=iasp91&evdepth=0&phase=S,SKS,SKIKS&stadepth=0&x=rayparam&y=amp&format=svg
